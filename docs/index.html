Trivia!(CS Addition)

Compete to earn the most points by answering 15 multiple choice questions of increasing difficulty.

You will have 3 cheats avaiable to you to use only once throughout the game:

  1. Take 2: Eliminate two of the four options.
  2. Jump: Skip the current question and the money associated with it.
  3. Swap: Swap out the current question with another of equal value.
